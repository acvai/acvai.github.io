About Me

Ph.D. candidate at the University of Burgundy, specializing in Human Action Recognition, embedded systems, and medical imaging AI. I build optimized AI models for real-time applications across FPGA, CPU, and GPU environments.

Reviewer at Elsevier, and contributor to EU-funded research projects. Passionate about innovation in AI, hardware acceleration, and image processing.

Research Projects

  • SIRFusion: Fusion of 3D skeletal and infrared data for improved action recognition.
  • ImpSGN: Lightweight GCN model optimized for FPGA using Xilinx Vitis-AI.
  • ResGCN-T: CNN-GCN architecture combining efficiency and accuracy for embedded platforms.
  • Mamba SSM Segmentation: Developing novel segmentation models for medical imaging based on state-space models.
